About Our Budget Builder App

Our Story & Mission

We built this app because we know firsthand how challenging budgeting can be. Our mission is simple: create a budgeting tool that we’d want to use ourselves. Inspired by personal struggles with managing finances—especially during unpredictable times—we set out to develop a straightforward, effective way to take control of your money. As college students, we understand the pressure of balancing limited resources, and we’re dedicated to making budgeting accessible for everyone.

Core Features & Benefits

  • User-Friendly Budget Creation: Easily build and adjust your budget with intuitive tools.
  • Expense Tracking: Monitor your spending in real time, helping you stay on target.
  • Customizable Categories: Personalize your budget by setting up categories that reflect your lifestyle.
  • Practical Design: Designed with simplicity in mind so you can focus on your financial goals.

Meet the Team

  • Jagger Zlotoff: A college student who has experienced the ups and downs of budgeting. His firsthand experience with financial challenges is the driving force behind this project.
  • David Jones: Also a college student, David is committed to making this app not just functional but engaging and intuitive. Together, they are passionate about building a tool that genuinely helps people manage their money better.

Security & Privacy

At this stage, nothing is saved on our website, ensuring that your personal information remains completely secure. Our focus is on providing you with a reliable budgeting tool without any worries about data breaches.

Future Roadmap

  • Projected Inflation Option: Imagine being able to adjust your budget for projected inflation—this could help you plan for future financial shifts.
  • Financial News Ticker: With the economy taking a downturn and more people needing to budget wisely, we’re exploring the idea of integrating a financial news ticker to keep you informed of the latest market trends.
